# PRODUCT.md — VEGA Holdings

## Product Purpose
Marketing landing page for VEGA Holdings — an Israeli staffing, cleaning, maintenance, and facility management company serving institutional, public, and private sector clients since 2000. The site communicates trust, scale, and operational reliability to both business clients and job seekers.

## Register
brand

## Users
- **Business clients**: facility managers, HR directors, property owners looking for staffing, cleaning, or facility management services
- **Job seekers**: workers looking for employment in cleaning, maintenance, security, hospitality
- **Returning visitors**: existing clients and employees checking job listings

## Brand Voice
Direct, credible, warm. Not corporate-cold. Speaks like a reliable partner, not a salesperson.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages with gradient text and hero-metric templates
- Glassmorphism, neon, dark-mode defaults
- Overcrowded layouts with every section competing for attention
- Tourist-brochure teal (this is a business services brand)
- Hebrew sites that feel like translated English — the copy should feel native

## Strategic Principles
- Seniority signals trust: 25+ years, 1000+ workers, nationwide coverage
- Human photography over stock-photo generic; the video hero is the emotional anchor
- Job listings are a primary conversion goal alongside contact inquiries
- The site is primarily Hebrew RTL; English is secondary
